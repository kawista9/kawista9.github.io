How to Make a Yummy Sandwhich!!

  1. Get the mayonaise, ham and cheese! Bread and knife!
  2. Put plate and other stuff on counter
  3. Open the jar of mayonnaise and use knife to scoop and spread on bread!
  4. When finished with mayo. lick the knife and close jar.
  5. Put meat and cheese in mircowave for 45 seconds.
  6. Place meat and cheese onto bread!
  7. Put the second slice of braid on top of the slice with meat and cheese
  8. Take a yummy bite!
  9. Take a nap!
